Abhyudaya
Our venture “Abhyudaya” was a spin-off of INTEL YOUTH ENTERPRISE - 2102 buisness plan competition. Abhyudaya was incubated by NSRCEL of IIMB and INTEL. Our venture aims at mitigating financial risk for farmers arising due to weather and market unpredictability. We developed an online web portal through which the farmers and traders could interact, also implemented daily weather reporting engine which would send weather updates to farmers.
